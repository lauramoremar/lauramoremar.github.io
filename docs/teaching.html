Teaching

Teaching overview

My teaching experience includes undergraduate and master’s courses in applied mathematics, numerical methods and computational mechanics at the University of Alicante, the University of Padova, Technische Universität Braunschweig and the Universitat Oberta de Catalunya. I have taught in Spanish, English and Italian, in both face-to-face and online settings, and I currently coordinate an undergraduate mathematics course in Aerospace Engineering.
